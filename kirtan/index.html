This page serves as a simple personal songbook of kirtan chants collected and composed over time. It's a resource for remembering the words and chords to various songs, including well-known classics. The accompanying audio samples are quick, one-take recordings intended as a memory aid, not good performances. They provide a brief overview of each chant's core structure to help recall the full spontaneous versions. Enjoy if it can be of any use to you as well.

Ong Namo Gurudev Namo

Gurudev Gurudev Namo, Gurudev Gurudev Namo

The Adi Mantra Ong is "Infinite Creative energy in manifestation and activity". ("Om" or Aum is God absolute and unmanifested), Namo is "reverent greetings' implying humility, Guru means "teacher or wisdom", Dev means "Divine or of God" and Namo reaffirms humility and reverence. In all it means, "I call upon Divine Wisdom".
