How to Use

Microsoft Edge is required for the intended high-quality text-to-speech voice.

The 'Read Aloud' feature must be manually triggered once before starting a session. This is a Microsoft requirement to enable the high-quality TTS engine.

On MOBILE Microsoft Edge:

  1. Click the ‚ò∞ menu and select 'Read Aloud'.
  2. The TTS engine will start. Immediately press pause.
  3. Choose a 5, 7, or 10-minute session to begin.

On DESKTOP Microsoft Edge:

  1. Press CTRL+SHIFT+U to enable the TTS engine.
  2. The TTS engine will start. Immediately press pause.
  3. Click a button to begin your meditation.

Enjoy your moment of mindfulness!
